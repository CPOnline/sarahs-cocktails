Planters Punch

Said to have been invented in the 1650's.
This is by far the earliest cocktail to emerge, and long before cocktails
became the in drink at bars and parties. Some say it originated in Jamaica, others
say America. Either way we say thank you to the magical mind that dreamed up the Planters Punch.

What you'll need

  • 30ml dark rum

  • 30ml light rum

  • 15ml orange curacao

  • 50ml orange juice

  • 50ml pineapple juice

  • 15ml honey

  • 1x fresh lime

  • A dash of grenadine

  • A dash of bitters

  • A slice of orange peel and a cherry

  • 15ml water

    • Step by step...

    1. Add the first five ingredients to an ice-filled shaker and shake well.

    2. Heat the honey and water in a saucepan and stir until a consistent, runny paste.
    3. Then add it into the mixture with the juice from the lime, grenadine and bitters.

    4. Stir well.

    5. Strain into a glass, garnish with orange peel and cherry.

    6. Enjoy while turning your mind to the sound of lilting waves or hum of city life.
